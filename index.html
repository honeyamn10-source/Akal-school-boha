About Akal School

Akal School Boha was established in 2000 and is managed by a private trust. Located in urban Boha, block Budhlada, district Mansa, Punjab.

  • Grades: 1 to 12 (State Board, PSEB)
  • Medium: Punjabi · Co-educational
