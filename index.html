About the school

Akal School Boha was established in 2000 and is managed by a private trust (Others). Located in the urban area of Boha, block Budhlada, district Mansa, Punjab.

  • Grades: 1 to 12 (State Board)
  • Medium: Punjabi · Co-educational
  • Head teacher: CHIRINJEEV SINGH
  • Faculty: 14 teachers (4 male, 10 female, 2 pre-primary)
  • Infra: 12 classrooms, pucca building, playground, library (215 books)
  • UDISE code: 03150201811

Mission & Vision

"Empowering rural students through value-based education in Punjabi, preserving culture while embracing modernity."

Principal's message

"At Akal School, every child is nurtured to excel academically and morally. Our dedicated staff ensures holistic growth." – CHIRINJEEV SINGH
